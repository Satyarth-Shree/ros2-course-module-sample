The Problem We Solve

Why Written Modules Matter

📉 High Drop-off After Workshops

Students who attend video-based courses lack persistent reference material — leading to confusion and drop-off when they practice independently.

🔍 Search-Friendly, Always Available

Written tutorials are searchable at any time. Students jump to the exact concept they need without scrubbing through a video.

⚡ Reinforces Video Learning

These modules complement KarthiKesh's video curriculum — not replace it. Students watch to learn, then read to retain and reference.
